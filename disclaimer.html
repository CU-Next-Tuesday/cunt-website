Methodology

What this site is — and what it isn’t

Updated continuously · Receipts-first

Short version

cunexttuesday.org documents public claims and public actions using primary sources. Opinions are labeled. Quotes are verbatim. Dates matter. If something can’t be sourced, it doesn’t get published.

Principle: Memory beats spin. Receipts beat vibes.

Methodology

  • Primary sources first. Video, transcripts, court filings, legislation, official statements, roll-call votes.
  • Verbatim quotes. No paraphrasing inside quotation marks. Edits are marked.
  • Timelines over takes. When events span time, we show the order they happened.
  • Clear labels. Commentary is labeled as commentary. Documentation is labeled as documentation.
  • Permanent links. Stable URLs are favored; archived copies are used when needed.

Corrections

If a factual error is found, it will be corrected transparently. Corrections note what changed and when. Silent edits are avoided.

What this site is not

  • Not a newsroom or a wire service.
  • Not a platform for anonymous tips without documentation.
  • Not interested in laundering talking points or “both-sides” framing.

Legal disclaimer (plain English)

This site publishes commentary and analysis based on publicly available information. It does not provide legal advice. Links to external sites are provided for reference; their content is owned by their respective publishers.

Public figures are discussed in their professional capacity, using their own words and documented actions. Intent is not asserted without evidence.
