Welcome to cunexttuesday.org

2026-01-13 · ~2 min read

This blog is intentionally boring under the hood: plain HTML + CSS. Cloudflare Pages serves it fast, cheaply, and reliably.

Why static?

It’s simple, secure, and doesn’t need a database. Adding a post is just adding a new file in /posts/ and linking it from /index.html.

What’s next

If you later want an editor, tags, search, or comments, you can add those without replatforming.
