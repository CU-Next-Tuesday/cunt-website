“We never supported that.” A case study.

2026-01-20 · ~4 min read · lies → reality

Claim: “We never supported that.”
Reality: The public record shows repeated support, followed by a rewrite once consequences appeared.
Bottom line: Memory collapses when receipts are optional. They aren’t optional here.

Key points

  • Denial comes after the policy or position becomes politically toxic.
  • The earlier support is documented in real time.
  • The rewrite relies on short attention spans.
