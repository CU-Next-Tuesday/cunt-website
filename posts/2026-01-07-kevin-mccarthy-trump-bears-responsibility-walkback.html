What was said

On January 13, 2021, House GOP Leader Kevin McCarthy said President Trump “bears responsibility” for the January 6 attack and should have acted to stop it.

What happened next

Within weeks, public posture shifted toward party management: keep the base, keep the conference, keep the ladder. The “spine check” is not about a single sentence — it’s about what followed.

Bottom line

Accountability was said out loud. Then it was functionally shelved. Ledger entry.
